History

 

The above parts are what I call

“The Story of Me”

All what The Lord has shown me is what you will find in the site.

Matthew 14:22-32

 

"the Storm"

I was once riding in a horse driven chariot. When a storm came and blew the chariot away. I started walking as I watched the flowers wither, and ground crack. The rain started to fall, and fall, and fall. The dust covered ground is now a mud slide. I struggle to stand and a river of water washes me off my feet. The water pushes me into a dark hole. I franticly try to climb out, but the roots brake in my hands and the sides are much to slick. I can see the sky clear, and the sun arise from down below. People I thought I knew came to the mouth of the hole. They tell lies of me as they look at one another and laugh. I am in shock as I lean against the wall of the hole. Speechless, I slide to my knees. The sky becomes dark, and there is nothing to do but wait.

 

The Lord first told me of this organization again about 20 years ago.

 

All my life The Lord has shown me things in the spirit. Once again, my life seemed have turned upside down. So I think what now, what direction should I take. I had always knew The Lord would use me for something in this world. I felt He would explain it to me when he decided to. It was at this point my life I had to know.

 

There was a small long dirt road, deep in the woods, with nothing around. I had gone here many times, to just sit in and talk with The Lord. This time I came for answers. I had thought about all the things knew or thought I knew, concerning my abilities, my current wealth and such. I explained them to The Lord. A college graduate and a decorated soldier.

 

I prayed in this place day after day, pouring out my heart, "Lord What Will You Do With Me" over and over, in tears. I would come back day after day, determined to find an answer. This went on for approximately 7 days. I can not be sure, this was a high emotional time.

 

I am hit with a heavy force, slamming into my head. I seen image after image, one right after another, awake my eyes open. These images were passing in my mind. So many, I could not count them and all in the time of half of one second. All I could remember was the first image and the last.

 

I seen the first image ten years ago, It was an important email I had sent. The last image was of me standing at the end of a board room table, my hands on table. Sitting at the table, to my front and right, was a man. To my front and left was a female in a chair, all I could see is her silhouette. Behind me was the glass of a tall building, about 80 floors. Through the windows I see another tall building, with the same height, to my back left and side. I see no other tall buildings, only small ones, all the way to a large lake about 1 kilometer away.

 

The Lord speaks through the last image. He said two things, “We Own the Building” and “Your on borrowed Time”. The “Your on borrowed Time” meant that when ever this last image is unveiled, He will return for his children.

Matthew 24:36

 

After seeing this, my first words were, “I cannot do this alone”.

 

Over the next 20 years The Lord explains to me how He will use us to change this world. He goes in great detail about many areas of existence the physical and the spiritual. He explained the origination, He would create. He gave me the name of Midnight Its Almost.

 

During the last 20 years took everything and everyone out of my life He did not want there. I lost everything I considered wealth. This happen so that I would rely on Him and not my own ability.
