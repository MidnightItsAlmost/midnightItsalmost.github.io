Products are generally sold over the internet. In many areas of the world we have display areas. At these display areas we have registration terminals. We either ship to the registrant’s address or to the location of the display area.

 

Display areas can be in established stores, or in intermodal containers at various locations. Within these areas products are for demonstration only.

 

At our registration terminals, all available products are cataloged. A registrant may review all their account activity as well as fund transfers.
