“Love Glasses”

 

Have you ever seen these Video Games today, or animated movies? This digital world, is a geographical computerized representation of Land, Mountains, Water, Buildings, Animals, People, Rooms, and everything else.

 

There are many different types of lasers, for detecting heat (infrared) and depth. Lasers do not work underwater. Underwater we use Sonar to detect depth and distances from one object to another.

 

From these two devices we can map our world.

This is Not a New Idea!

It is just that the tools have not been placed in the right hands.

 

Imagine a pair of glasses, the really cool looking ones. The lenses of these glasses are not made glass, they a woven mesh of scion fiber (Fiber Optics). This mesh can make a curved digital screen. One screen to the inside, and one screen to the out side. Two separate screens.

 

Along the top frame of the glasses, are lasers to record distances. In this same area there are sonar transmitters and receivers, and normal digital cameras. All these instruments in the frame are very small, the size of pebbles.

 

Now to put this tool in the right hands.

 

These glasses can replace prescription glasses. The focus of the images recorded by the digital cameras can be adjusted to accommodate (fit to the need of) the users vision.

 

Using the inferred lasers, the user can see in complete darkness, night vision. Imagine being able to see when walking through a parking lot at night, or any other dark place.

 

The outside screens can display any video or still image. You can play a Music Video of your favorite band on the glasses you are wearing for all to see, or a picture of “anything”. Think about it.

 

The inside screen can be connected to any video game, movie or computer. These would be displayed in 3D.

 

At all times these glasses are recording Width, Depth, and Height, of the world around them. This is the geographical computerized representation. This is a world of data, computer code. The data is then uploaded to the internet through Wi-Fi by way of cellular communications joining the data of the digital world.

 

It is unknown how long it would take to complete this digital world, and I feel it would never be fully completed. None the less what will be there will be more than enough to evaluate (test) weather, to direct air travel, direct shipping, geological information (Earthquake data), and so much more.

 

Within this digital map you can see another part of the world as it is being recorded. For instance, your friend/family gets a new home in Germany and wants to show it to you. You are both talking on the phone. You are in Russia, your friend/family walks through the entire house while you see everything they do, In Real Time.

 

I know what you are thinking. This can be done with any cellular phone. Yes, but these glasses do so much more.

 

“Saints, 144,000”

We will use these glasses to create the “wire frames” needed to build the land base environment.

 

PlanetSide2 is one I play, I like this game

 

 

The above shows different make believe digital worlds. All Vehicles, and people you see, are someone playing on a computer somewhere. No Bots (a Bot is a computer generated player) Bots are easy, people are hard.

 

The game we intend to create, will be called “Saints, 144,000”. This new game will be played within the Digital World, as mapped by the glasses.

 

Blow up you home town, or that over high priced store you love to hate. This entices people to use those Glasses I told you of. Children are always full of energy. Think how fast they could map this world.

 

IonUThe Security Division of Midnight its, Almost drones also travel day and night, mapping everything that has not been previously mapped. Their power supply is the electrical engine so they never stop. MIA controls access to this world.
