World Development

 

It is our intention to lease areas of land within countries such as these.

 

Madagascar, Eritrea, Guinea, Mozambique, Malawi, Niger, Liberia, Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Central African Republic.

 

We make a contract with the governing parties of these countries. We abide by these laws Exodus 20:1-17 and any international laws that do not conflict with the laws written in the Bible. We will have the right to protect these areas with lethal force as long as the contract with the governing parties of that country is honored.

 

Within these areas we build a school, a hospital, housing complexes, agricultural structures, a bible based church, manufacturing plants, and an airport. Around the perimeter of these areas there will be a wall for protection from small arms fire.

 

The first few areas will be the hardest to make and most expensive to establish. Previously trained civil, architectural, mechanical, and electrical engineers will need to be hired to direct and oversee the construction of these areas. While these first areas are being developed local inhabitants will be trained, in these areas of expertise. After which the newly qualified personnel will direct and oversee the construction of new areas in different countries.

 

We will ship products from these areas to distribution location around the world.
