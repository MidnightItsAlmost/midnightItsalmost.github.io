Organizational Structure and Policies

Purchasing Power

 

Purchasing Power Amount (PPA)

Is the sum amount paid by the registered user for any good or service provided by Midnight Its Almost (MIA). The minimum Purchasing Power Amount (PPA) is this sum of all purchases over the previous one year. The maximum Purchasing Power Amount (PPA) is the sum of all purchases over the previous three years

 

Loan Amount (LA)

The Loan Amount (LA) is the Purchasing Power Amount (PPA) that can be loaned to a registered user to obtain a good or service provided by MIA.

 

Repayment Amount (RA)

The repayment amount (RA) is the Loaned Amount (LA) multiplied by one point one (10%), and done so only once.

 

Example LA * 1.1 = RA

 

Additional Percentage Cost (APC)

The Additional Percentage Cost (APC) is the Loaned Amount (LA) divided by the Purchasing Power Amount (PPA).

 

Example RA / PPA = APC

 

Good or Service Cost (GSC)

The Good or Service Cost (GSC) is the monetary value of any good or service provided by MIA.

 

Adjusted Good or Service Cost (AGSC)

The Adjusted Good or Service Cost (AGSC) is the Good or Service Cost (GSC) multiplied by the Additional Percentage Cost (APC) then added to the original Good or Service Cost (GSC).

 

Example: GSC * (1 + APC) = AGSC

 

The Adjusted Good or Service Cost (AGSC) is required by the registered user to pay off the Loan Amount (LA).

 

Usage shown below

Repayment Formula

 

LA * 1.10 = RA

 

RA / PPA = APC

 

GSC * (1 + APC) = AGSC

 

Practical Example

LA = 300 and PPA = 1000

LA * 1.1 = RA

300 * 1.1 = 330

RA = 330

The Registrant has been given a loan of 300. The Registrant will pay back 330.

 

RA / PPA = APC

330 / 1000 = 0.33

APC = 0.33

 

GSC = 5

This is the cost of a product or one of the payments of a service.

 

GSC * (1 + APC) = AGSC

5 * (1 + 0.33) = 6.65

AGSC = 6.65

This is how much more the product will cost or how much more one payment will be.

 

New RA = 328.35

The loan will be reduced by 1.65, in this example the remaining loan amount will be 328.35.
